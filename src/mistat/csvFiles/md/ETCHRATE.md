Data on the Rate of Etching
===========================

Description
-----------

Rate of removal of field oxide in a semiconductor plasma etching
process.

Usage
-----

    data(ETCHRATE)

Source
------

Digital Equipment Corporation (1988).

Examples
--------

    data(ETCHRATE)

    hist(ETCHRATE)
